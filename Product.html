How It Works

  • Bamboo: Extracts soil from below.
  • Charcoal, Sand, Cotton/Coffee Filter: Filters the soil.
  • pH Indicator: Detects acidity levels.
  • Nitrogen Indicator: Detects nitrates to assess soil fertility.
  • Any Wick Material: Absorbs water upward.
  • Plastic: Covers and protects the product.
  • Paper: Indicates results through color change.

Process Steps

  1. Soil and water enter from the bottom.
  2. The soil is filtered, leaving clean water.
  3. The clean water triggers the pH and nitrogen indicator papers.
  4. The paper changes color, providing the final result.
